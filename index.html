Tribute to Andremma Garstone

A timeline to honour the relationship of Andrew Garfield and Emma Stone

Here's a timeline of their relationship:

  • October 2010 - Emma and Andrew start filming The Amazing Spiderman.
  • April/May 2011 - Emma and Andrew split from their respective boyfriend/girlfriend.
  • November 2011 - The two are yet to make their relationship status known. Emma hosts Saturday Night Live and Andrew makes a suprise guest appearance (fans are suspicious).
  • March 2012 - Andrew and Emma are spotted kissing in the streets of New York. (Reddit threads absolutely freak out)
  • April 2012 - Emma and Andrew walk the red carpet together at the Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards.
  • June 2012 - Press tour of The Amazing Spiderman. Emma and Andrew are adorable. Everyone has forgotten about Toby Maguire and Kirsten Dunst.
  • March 2014 - The Amazing Spiderman 2 press tour leaves Jamie Foxx as the awkward third wheel.
  • April 2015 - Andremma Garstone officially becomes Andrew Garfield and Emma Stone.
  • December 2016 - In an interview, Andrew Garfield is asked who he would take to a desert island. He says "Emma Stone. I love Emma." Emma Stone says in an interview that she is single.
  • January 2017 - They reuinte at the Golden Globes. Andrew gives Emma a standing ovation when she wins 'Best Actress'.
  • January 2022 - Andrew and Emma are still great friends. But not close enough for Emma to know the details of the new Spiderman film.

"Emma kept on texting and she was like, ‘Are you in this new ‘Spider-Man’ film?’ And I was like, ‘I don’t know what you’re talking about.'"

-- Andrew Garfield
